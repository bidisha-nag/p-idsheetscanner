About

The system is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline the processing of PDF documents by automatically detecting and highlighting predefined symbols within them. Users can upload PDF files and a corresponding zip archive containing symbol images. Upon processing, the system identifies matches between the symbols and the content of the PDF, displaying the results efficiently. With its intuitive interface and advanced recognition algorithms, it enhances productivity and accuracy in tasks such as content analysis, data extraction, and pattern recognition, catering to various industries and research fields requiring efficient document analysis and information extraction capabilities.
